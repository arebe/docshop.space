What is DocShop?

University life is siloed.
Faculty are situated in different schools while fellows and students flow in and out of Boston each year, displaced and dispersed until they can one day carve out space for their own work.

DocShop brings these groups together through the workshopping and exhibition of intereactive media. It exists as a collaboratory, facilitating production of works; as a production engine for events with artists; as a think tank and research cohort.

Emanating from the Berkman Center's Digital Problem Solving Initiative, DocShop is situated at Harvard's metaLab and includes members from groups around Harvard and MIT.

DocShop will challenge the role of author and audience in convivial and participatory workshops, installations/exhibitions, and events.
