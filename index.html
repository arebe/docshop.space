What is DocShop?

University life is siloed. Faculty are situated in different schools, conducting research in their own labs. Over 500 accomplished Fellows flow in and out of greater Boston each year without producing new work. Students are left to fit into to other peopleâ€™s agenda, paying their dues until they can one day carve out space for their own work.

DocShop is a collaboratory that incubates artist projects, stages events, and installs interactive media to create an expressive community. It will exist as this collaboratory, facilitating production of works, in addition to project-based work on events with artist, and as a think tank or research cohort.

DocShop will incubate the work of artists along with challenging the role of author and audience in convivial and participatory workshops, installations/exhibitions, and events.
